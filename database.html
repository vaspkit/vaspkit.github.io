2D Semiconductor Database¶

Recently We screened around 300 two dimensional nonmagnetic semiconductors from near 1000 monolayers according to the energetic, thermodynamic, mechanical, dynamic and thermal stability criterions. We will present the calculated results including lattice constants, formation energy, Young’s modulus, Poisson’s ratio, shear modulus, band gap, band structure, ionization energy and electron affinity for all the candidates satisfying our criteria [Come Soon].

For more details of the code, please refer to the appendix of the following reference.

[1] V. Wang, Y.-Y. Liang, Y. Kawazeo, W.-T. Geng, High-Throughput Computational Screening of Two-Dimensional Semiconductors, arXiv:1806.04285.
