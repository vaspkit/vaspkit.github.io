ATOMKIT Code
============
The VASPKIT team is actively developing an innovative and practical software tool called ATOMKIT. Our long-term goal is to transform ATOMKIT into a powerful and versatile crystal structure modeling software toolkit that can run across different platforms to meet various research needs.
